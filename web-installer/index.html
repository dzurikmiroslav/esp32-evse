esp32-evse installer

  1. Connect the EVSE board to your computer using USB or serial-to-USB adapter
  2. Select your EVSE board
  3. Press Connect button and select corret serial port
  4. Confirm install in dialog, some boards must be manualy switched to flashing mode by holding WiFi buton while reset (ESP32-S2 EVSE DIY ALPHA)
  5. After install, press WiFi button on board and connect to WiFi evse-XXXXXX, then open url http://192.168.4.1
